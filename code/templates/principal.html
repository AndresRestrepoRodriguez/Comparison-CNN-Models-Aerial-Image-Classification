Welcome


This application aims to provide an intuitive web environment to carry out the comparison of convolutional neural network models, focused on the classification of satellite scenes taking classes such as: Airplane, Airport, Commercial Area, Forest, Highway, Island, Ship and Stadium. The comparison can be made with its own model compared to pre-trained models such as MobileNet, ResNet50, MobileNetV2 and InceptionV3, or only among pre-trained models. Finally, the software performs the comparison and sends a report with the evaluation of metrics such as: Roc curves, F1 score, redial score, precision score and Kappa coefficient.

About Dataset


This dataset is made up of a total of 4130 images distributed in eight classes: Airplane, Airport, Commercial Area, Forest, Freeway, Island, Ship and Stadium. These images were taken from the NWPU-RESISC45 dataset
